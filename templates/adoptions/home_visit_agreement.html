Home Visit Agreement

Last Updated: May 20, 2026

1. Purpose of Home Visit

The home visit is conducted to ensure that the adopted pet will be placed in a safe, loving, and suitable environment. This is a mandatory step in the adoption process for first-time adopters.

2. Scheduling

  • Home visits will be scheduled within 14 days of application approval
  • You will be contacted to arrange a convenient date and time
  • Please provide at least 24 hours notice if you need to reschedule

3. What to Expect

A shelter representative will:

  • Inspect all areas where the pet will live (indoors and outdoors)
  • Check for potential hazards (toxic plants, exposed wires, open windows)
  • Verify that fencing and gates are secure
  • Discuss pet care responsibilities and expectations
  • Answer any questions you may have

4. Requirements

To pass the home visit, you must demonstrate:

  • Safe and clean living environment
  • Adequate space for the pet's size and activity level
  • Secure fencing or containment for outdoor areas
  • No aggressive pets currently in the home
  • All household members agree to the adoption

5. After the Visit

Following the home visit:

  • You will receive a pass/fail result within 48 hours
  • If you pass, you may proceed with the adoption
  • If you fail, you will receive specific reasons and recommendations
  • You may reapply after addressing any issues (minimum 30 days)

6. Your Agreement

By registering as an adopter, you agree to:

  • Allow a shelter representative to conduct a home visit
  • Provide access to all areas where the pet will live
  • Follow any safety recommendations made during the visit
  • Notify the shelter if your living situation changes significantly

7. Cost

Home visits are provided free of charge as part of the adoption process. No payment is required for this service.
