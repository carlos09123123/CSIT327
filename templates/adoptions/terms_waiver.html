Adoption Waiver & Terms of Service

Important: Please read this document carefully before submitting an adoption application.

1. Adoption Requirements

By applying to adopt a pet from our shelter, you confirm that:

  • You are at least 18 years of age.
  • You have a stable home environment suitable for the pet.
  • You have the financial means to provide proper care, including food, veterinary visits, and supplies.
  • You understand that adoption is a lifelong commitment (10-20 years depending on the pet).
  • All members of your household agree to the adoption.

2. Pet Health Disclosure

The shelter provides all known medical history for each pet, including:

  • Vaccination records
  • Medical treatments received
  • Known health conditions
  • Spay/neuter status

However, adopters acknowledge that there may be unknown health conditions and agree to have the pet examined by a licensed veterinarian within 7 days of adoption.

3. Adoption Fee

The adoption fee is non-refundable and covers:

  • Spaying/neutering (if applicable)
  • Initial vaccinations
  • Microchipping
  • Health check-up
  • Deworming
  • Flea/tick treatment

4. Return Policy

If for any reason you cannot keep the adopted pet, you agree to:

  • Contact the shelter immediately at (032) 123-4567
  • Return the pet to the shelter (no questions asked)
  • Not sell, give away, or abandon the pet
  • Provide a written explanation for the return

The shelter reserves the right to deny future adoptions to individuals who return pets without valid reason.

5. Home Visit Agreement

You agree to:

  • Allow a shelter representative to conduct a home visit
  • Provide access to all areas where the pet will live
  • Follow any recommendations made during the visit
  • Schedule the visit within 14 days of application approval

6. Post-Adoption Follow-Up

You agree to:

  • Provide updates and photos of the adopted pet
  • Notify the shelter of any veterinary concerns
  • Keep the pet indoors or in a secure area
  • Provide proper identification (ID tag, microchip registration)

7. Liability Waiver

Release of Liability:
To the fullest extent permitted by law, you release the shelter, its employees, volunteers, and agents from any and all liability, claims, demands, actions, or causes of action whatsoever arising out of or related to any loss, damage, or injury, including death, that may be sustained by you, your family, your pets, or any third party resulting from the adoption.

8. Indemnification

You agree to indemnify and hold harmless the shelter from any claims, damages, or expenses arising from your adoption of the pet, including but not limited to:

  • Property damage caused by the pet
  • Injuries caused by the pet to any person or animal
  • Violation of local animal ordinances

9. Acknowledgment

By checking the agreement box on the application form, you acknowledge that:

  • You have read and understand this waiver and terms of service
  • The information you provided is true and accurate
  • You agree to be bound by these terms
  • You understand that false information may result in application rejection
  • You consent to the shelter contacting your references

10. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of the Philippines. Any disputes arising from this adoption shall be resolved exclusively in the courts of Cebu City.
